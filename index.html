Best SPAs in Hoi An

Where can I get the best massage and treatment in Vietnam?



Citrus Health Spa


Citrus is probably the best spa in Hoi An. They have gained a reputation for the best massages in Hoi An and are widely regarded as one of Hoi An's premier destinations for beauty and healing therapy.
Citrus Spa boasts a team of highly skilled massage therapists who are dedicated to providing the best care to every customer.
Crafted with expertise and attention to detail, Citrus Spa's signature massage oils are infused with exquisite essential oils.
The spa's treatments are designed with a deep understanding of human anatomy and physiology, delivering therapeutic benefits that rejuvenate both body and mind.

Coral Spa


At Coral Spa Hoian they have perfected the art of body treatment. It is located very close to old town and they offer a large range of therapies, including exfoliating scrubs and nourishing wraps. Their body treatments aim to cleanse, rejuvenate, and invigorate the skin, leaving guests feeling refreshed and renewed.
Upon arrival, guests are welcomed into a serene environment, designed to evoke a sense of tranquility and relaxation. The ambiance itself plays a crucial role in preparing individuals for the transformative experience that awaits them. Soft lighting, calming scents, and soothing music create an atmosphere conducive to letting go of stress and embracing the present moment.

Close to old town

La Spa by La Siesta


Located in the La Siesta resort, La Spa offers a large range of treatments and therapies including Swedish therapy, hot stones, Himalayan salt stone and herbal massages and a range of foot care and facial treatments. Body wraps incorporating a number of natural ingredients such as coffee, seaweed, olive oil and fresh milk. They also have a selection of 2 to 4 hour packages for longer pampering.
