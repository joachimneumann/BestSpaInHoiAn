Citrus Health Spa

Probably the best Spa in Hoi An


Citrus has gained a reputation for the best massages in Hoi An and are widely regarded as one of Hoi An's premier destinations for beauty and healing therapy. Citrus Spa boasts a team of highly skilled massage therapists who are dedicated to providing the best care to every customer.

The massage at Citrus Health Spa

Crafted with expertise and attention to detail, Citrus Spa's signature massage oils are infused with exquisite essential oils. The spa's treatments are designed with a deep understanding of human anatomy and physiology, delivering therapeutic benefits that rejuvenate both body and mind.

Coral Spa


Coral Spa in Hoi An, provides spa services in a calm and inviting atmosphere. Coral Spa is located very close to the Hoi An old town. They offer a large range of therapies, including exfoliating scrubs and nourishing wraps. Their body treatments aim to cleanse, rejuvenate, and invigorate the skin, leaving guests feeling refreshed and renewed. Upon arrival, guests are welcomed into a serene environment, designed to evoke a sense of tranquility and relaxation. The ambiance itself plays a crucial role in preparing individuals for the transformative experience that awaits them. Soft lighting, calming scents, and soothing music create an atmosphere conducive to letting go of stress and embracing the present moment.

La Spa by La Siesta


Located in the La Siesta resort, La Spa offers a large range of treatments and therapies including Swedish therapy, hot stones, Himalayan salt stone and herbal massages and a range of foot care and facial treatments.
